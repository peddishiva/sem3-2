Week 1 – UiPath Installation Steps
Open your browser and go to UiPath Cloud or the UiPath start page.
Click Sign Up if you are a new user, or Log In if you already have an account.
Create your UiPath account using email or Google.
If asked, create your organization in UiPath Automation Cloud.
In the dashboard, find Studio and click Download Studio.
After download completes, open the installer file.
Choose Community Edition if you are using the free student version.
Select the installation type, usually Quick Install.
Accept the license agreement.
Click Install and wait for installation to finish.
After installation, click Launch UiPath Studio.
Sign in using the same UiPath account.
Verify that UiPath Studio opens successfully and is ready to create a project.
